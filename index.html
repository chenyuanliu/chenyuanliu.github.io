Chenyuan Liu

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Tsinghua University, School of Economics and Management. My research interests are in health economics, insurance markets, industrial organization and applied microeconomics more broadly. I received my Ph.D. in Business (Risk and Insurance) from the University of Wisconsin at Madison, and my Bachelor degree in Economics from the University of Hong Kong.
